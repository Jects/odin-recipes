Fantastic Fried Rice

Fried rice is a popular Asian dish that is made by stir-frying cooked rice with a variety of ingredients such as vegetables, meat, eggs, and spices.

Ingredients

  • Coooked Rice
  • Frozen Mixed Vegetables
  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Heat a large skillet or wok over high heat.
  2. Add a tablespoon of oil and swirl to coat.
  3. Add frozen mixed vegetables and stir-fry until tender.
  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and crack two eggs into the other side
  5. Scramble the eggs until cooked.
  6. Add cooked rice to the skillet and stir-fry until heated thrrough.
  7. Drizzle with soy sauce and sesame oil, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot.
