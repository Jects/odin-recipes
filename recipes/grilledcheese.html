Grandma's Grilled Cheese

A grilled cheese sandwich is a simple and classic comfort food that consists of toasted bread and melted cheese. To make a grilled cheese sandwich, you butter one side of two slices of bread, place a slice or two of cheese in between, and then grill the sandwich in a skillet or griddle until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted. The result is a warm and gooey sandwich that's perfect for a quick lunch or dinner.

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Heat a large skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place a slice of cheese between two slices of break, with the buttered sides facing out.
  4. Grill the saandwich until the bread is toasted and the cheese is melted.
  5. Serve hot.
